Spaghetti

Ingredients: 
Ground beef: 1 1/2 pounds
Salt: 1 1/2 teaspoon
Pepper: 1/2 teaspoon
Onion: 1 1/2 cups (chopped)
Green bell pepper: 1 1/2 cups (finely chopped)
Garlic: 1 tablespoon (minced)
Crushed tomatoes canned: 28 ounce
Tomato sauce: 16 ounce
Tomato paste: 6 ounce
Italian seasoning: 1 tablespoon
Suagar: 1/2 teaspoon
Spaghetti: 16 ounces

Garnish:
Parsley (finely chopped): 2 tablespoons
Grated parmesan: To taste

Instructions:
1. Heat a large pot over medium-high heat. Add beef to the pot, sprinkle with salt and pepper, and crumble into small chunks with a wooden spoon and cook until lightly browned. Use a slotted spoon to transfer to a plate
2. Drain all but 2 tablespoons of the fat from the pan and set over medium-low heat (if you don't have enough fat in the pan, add a tablespoon of olive oil). Add the onion, bell pepper, and garlic and cook, until onion is translucent, about 7 minutes
3. Return beef to pot then stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, and sugar. Bring sauce to a boil, then immediately reduce heat and simmer, uncovered for 1 hour. Adjust seasoning as needed
4. Meanwhile cook spaghetti in well-salted water, according to package instructions
5. Drain well and toss with sauce. Sprinkle with parsley and serve with parmesan
