Chicken Noodle Soup

Ingredients:
Unsalted butter: 2 tablespoons
Carrots (sliced): 1 cup
Celery(thinly sliced): 1 cup
Onions (finely chopped): 3/4 cup
Ginger (finely chopped): 1 tablespoon
Garlic(finely chopped): 1 tablespoon
Chicken broth (low sodium): 7.5 cups
Dried thyme: 1/2 teaspoon
Dried oregano: 1/2 teaspoon
Bay leaf: 1
Egg noodles: 12 oz
Shredded chicken: 1.5 cups
Fresh parsley (finely chopped): 2 tablespoons
Green onions: 1 table spoon
Salt and pepper: to taste

Instructions: 
1. In a large stockpot, heat butter on medium high heat
2. Add carrots, celery, onions and saute everything until the veggies become slightly tender
3. Add ginger, garlic and saute them for 1-2 minutes, making sure not to burn them
4. Add broth, thyme, oregano, bay leaf, salt, pepper and bring everything to a boil
5. Let it cook for another 5 minutes
6. Add egg noodles and let it all cook for about 10 minutes or until the noodles become soft
7. Add chicken and parsley and cook for another 3 minutes
8. Check the flavor to see if you need to add more herbs or spices and add accordingly
9. Discard the bay leaf and garnish with green onions and enjoy
