Butter Chicken

Chicken Marinade Ingredients:
Boneless chicken thighs (chopped into 1 inch cubes): 2 pounds
Grated garlic: 2 tablespoons
Grated ginger: 1 tablespoon
Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
Chili powder: 1 teaspoon
Tumeric: 1 teaspoon
Cumin: 1/2 teaspoon
Plain yogurt: 1/2 cup
Lemon juice: 1 table spoon
salt and pepper: To taste

Butter Chicken Ingredients:
Butter: 2 tablespoons
Medium onion: 1 (medium diced)
Red pepper: 1 (small dice)
Garlic cloves: 3 (minced)
Grated ginger: 1 tablespoon
Garam masala: 1 teaspoon
Cumin: 1 teaspoon
Red chili powder: 1 teaspoon
Diced tomatoes: 14 ounces
Heavy cream: 1 cup
Salt and pepper: To taste

For the chicken marinade:
1. Add the chopped chicken into a medium bowl. Add the garlic, ginger, garam masala, chili powder, tumeric, cumin, yogurt, lemon juice and salt and pepper
2. Mix well to coat and cover chicken. Cover and set in refridgerator to marinate for at least 30 minutes or overnight for best flavour.

For the Butter chicken: 
1.Heat a large pan to medium high heat and add the olive oil. Add the chicken and cook for 5 minutes, stirring, until the chicken is browned. Remove the chicken and set it aside.
2.Melt the butter in the same pan. Add the onion and peppers and cook them 5 minutes to soften.
3.Add the garlic and ginger and cook 1 minute, until they become fragrant.
4.Add the garam masala, cumin, red chili powder, salt and pepper. Stir and cook for 1 minute.
5.Stir in the diced tomatoes. Bring to a quick boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 15 minutes to break everything down.
6.Transfer the sauce to a blender or food processor and process until smooth. You can thin it out with a bit of water if youâ€™d like.
7.Strain the sauce back into the pan. The point is to make the sauce very smooth and heat through.
8.Stir in the cream and add the chicken back to the pan. Heat and simmer for 10 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the sauce thickens up a bit.
9.Serve with cooked white rice and enjoy!
