Seafood Sandwich (Lobster Roll)

Ingredients:
Cooked lobster meat: 3 1/2 cups (chopped into 3/4 inch pieces)
Mayonnaise: 1/4 cup
Lemon juice: 1 1/2 tablespoons
Celery: 1/4 cup (finely chopped)
Chives: 1 table spoon (thinly sliced)
Split top hot dog buns: 4
Butter (softened): 2 tablespoons
Salt and pepper: to taste

Instructions:
1. Place the lobster meat, mayonnaise, lemon juice, celery, chives, salt and pepper in a bowl. Stir gently to combine
2. Spread the butter onto the outer sides of each bun. Place the buns in a pan or on a griddle over medium heat. Cook for 2-3 minutes per side or until golden brown
3. Divide the lobster mixture among the buns. Serve immediately
