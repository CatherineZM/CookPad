Homemade Chicken Ramen

Ingredients: 
Vegetable Oil: 1 Tablespoon
Medium onion: 1 (finely diced)
Garlic cloves: 6 (finely diced)
Fresh ginger: 1 tablespoon (diced)
Chicken stock: 6 cups
Chili garlic sauce: 1 tablespoon
Soy sauce: 1/4 cup
Bok choy: 1-2 cups (finely chopped)
Shredden chicken (from rotisserie chicken): 2 1/2 cups
Ramen or Udon noodle Package: 1
Green onions: 1/2 cup (chopped)
Sliced mushrooms: 1/2 cup
Boiled egg: 1
Sriracha: to taste
Salt and pepper: to taste

Instructions
1. Heat a heavy-bottomed stockpot over medium-high heat. Add oil and onions and let cook for 5-6 minutes, or until onions begin to soften. Stir in garlic and ginger. Cook for only 1 minute longer
2. Add chicken broth. Stir in chili garlic sauce and soy sauce
3. Let simmer for at least 20 minutes on medium-low heat
4. While simmering, prepare noodles according to package instructions
5. Stir in bok choy, mushrooms and chicken. Season to taste
6. To serve, place cooked noodles on the bottom of the bowl. Ladle in chicken ramen over noodles. Garnish with green onions, mushrooms, boiled egg and sriracha
