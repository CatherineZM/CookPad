Homemade Pizza

Dough Ingredients:
Warm water (110 degrees F): 1 1/2 cups
Sugar: 2 teaspoons
Active dry yeast: 2 1/4 teaspoons
Bread flour: 3 1/2 cups
Olive oil: 2 tablespoons
Salt: 2 teaspoons

Pizza Ingredients:
Ball of dough: 1
Pizza sauce: 1/2 cup
Mozzarella cheese (grated): 2 cups
Button mushrooms (chopped): 1 cup
 
Instructions:
1. Combine warm water, sugar, and yeast in a large bowl. If using active dry yeast, let it proof for 5 minutes until foamy. 
2. Add flour, olive oil, and salt and stir well with a wooden spoon or mix in a stand mixer with a dough hook until combined.
3. Knead for 5 minutes using a stand mixer fitted with a dough hook attachment or 6-7 minutes by hand until smooth and elastic.
4. Drizzle a clean bowl with a little olive oil, then place the ball of dough in the bowl and turn it to coat in the oil. Cover with plastic wrap or a clean cloth and let rise for at least 30 minutes (or up to 1 1/2 hours) until doubled in size.
5. Divide the dough into 2 or 3 balls
6. Preheat oven to 500 degrees F and place a pizza stone or pan into oven while you make the pizza
7. Roll, pat, or stretch the dough out until it is roughly 1/4" thick 
8. Then top with 1/2 cup of pizza sauce, 2 cups mozzarella cheese and 1 cup button mushrooms
9. Bake for 7-10 minutes or until cheese is melted and the crust is golden brown and crispy around the edges.
10. Let pizza cool for 3-4 minutes before cutting and serving
